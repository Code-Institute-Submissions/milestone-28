Learning to swim

... is a life skill that can come in very useful throughout one’s life. Swimming is not only a great way to keep fit it is also a life saving skill. Swimming can make a whole range of water activities and water sports accessible, such as volleyball, surfing, kayaking or water polo. Learning to swim is also an important preventative measure towards accidental drowning.
